Trading with Rayner Teo

Role: Lead Editor, Options Trading Niche  |  Duration: 2023–2025

Overview

Rayner Teo runs one of the top-15 trading education blogs globally, with an audience of 210,000+ followers. I joined as lead editor with a focus on the options trading niche — the most technically demanding content the platform produces.

What I did

  • Edited and ghostwrote 500+ articles across options trading, technical analysis, chart patterns, trading psychology, and risk management
  • Verified mathematical frameworks — expectancy calculations, position sizing formulas, backtested strategy results, and option greeks

Why it mattered

Trading education content that gets the maths wrong damages credibility with exactly the audience that knows better. The editorial standard here required both genuine domain knowledge and the ability to write clearly for learners — not just one or the other.
