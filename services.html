Services

I work with trading educators, crypto platforms, and high-compliance businesses who need content that's technically accurate, editorially clean, and built to scale. Below is a breakdown of what I offer and who each service suits.

Trading Education Content

Best for: Trading educators, financial publishers, investment platforms

I write and edit trading and finance content to a standard that most generalist editors can't match — because I verify the underlying concepts, not just the prose. That means catching errors in option greeks, confirming backtesting methodology, and ensuring risk management frameworks are presented accurately.

What this covers:

  • Strategy guides — trend following, mean reversion, breakout systems
  • Technical analysis — chart patterns, indicators, price action
  • Options trading — calls, puts, spreads, greeks, strategies
  • Trading psychology and risk management frameworks
  • Mathematical content — expectancy, position sizing, backtesting results
  • Ghostwriting for educators and platform blogs

Format range: 300-word explainers to 4,000-word definitive guides.

AI-Augmented Content Systems

Best for: High-volume content operations, affiliate platforms, agencies scaling output

If you need consistent output at volume, I design and build the systems that make that possible — not just write individual pieces. I've built AI content pipelines from the ground up, including quality control frameworks, fact-checking protocols, and multi-stage review processes using modern LLMs.

What this covers:

  • Custom LLM pipeline design (GPT, Gemini, Claude)
  • Prompt engineering and workflow optimisation
  • Quality control and anti-plagiarism frameworks
  • Editorial review layers for AI-generated content
  • Python automation for content operations
  • Training and documentation for in-house teams

Output: A repeatable system your team can run, not just a one-off deliverable.

High-Compliance Content

Best for: iGaming operators, DeFi projects, regulated finance platforms, automotive

In sectors where regulatory accuracy matters, a missed detail is a real liability. I understand the compliance requirements that shape content in iGaming, DeFi, and other regulated spaces, and I edit accordingly — verifying licensing claims, flagging unsubstantiated statements, and ensuring content meets platform and jurisdictional standards.

What this covers:

  • Casino and sports betting reviews (licensing, bonus terms, regulatory claims)
  • DeFi and crypto content (protocol accuracy, risk disclosures)
  • Automotive (technical specifications, compliance terminology)
  • Regulatory and compliance editing across other verticals on request

SEO & Content Strategy

Best for: Content-led businesses, affiliate sites, trading and finance blogs

I think in content systems, not individual articles. That means planning heading hierarchies that capture featured snippets, building internal linking structures that distribute authority effectively, and designing content calendars that cover a topic cluster systematically rather than randomly.

What this covers:

  • Content audits and gap analysis
  • Topic cluster planning and content calendars
  • On-page SEO editing (structure, headings, meta, internal links)
  • Featured snippet and search intent optimisation
  • Blog strategy for trading, finance, and crypto platforms
  • Scaling from individual articles to 1,000+ piece operations
